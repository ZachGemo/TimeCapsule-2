This website was made in March 2019 and functions as a digital time capsule. The coordinates serve as directions to specific locations on the SUNY Purchase campus, unique to the four names you see displayed above. You may access each time capsule when you reach the respective destination. This is a collaborative project between Design for Screens with Kelsey Elder and Scripting for the Web with Lee Tusman, of SPRING 2019.
